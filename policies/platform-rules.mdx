These rules govern how GIS Agent may be used by all users, application creators, and service providers on the platform. By using GIS Agent, you agree to comply with these guidelines. Violations may result in content removal, account restrictions, or permanent account suspension.

---

## 1. General Provisions

### Purpose

These rules establish standards for operating and managing the GIS Agent platform. The goal is to support the platform's healthy development, protect users' lawful rights and interests, and ensure compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and policy requirements.

### Legal and Normative Basis

GIS Agent platform rules rest on three foundations:

- **Legal basis** — Applicable laws and regulations including the *Cybersecurity Law of the People's Republic of China*, the *Regulations on the Administration of Internet Information Services*, the *Interim Measures for the Administration of Generative Artificial Intelligence Services*, and the *Regulations on the Administration of Deep Synthesis on the Internet*, along with other relevant normative documents. These laws define the rights and obligations of all parties in the GIS Agent ecosystem and provide the legal basis for these rules.

- **Normative basis** — The GIS Agent platform agreements and terms of service serve as the normative foundation, clarifying the rights and responsibilities between GIS Agent and its users.

- **Guiding principles** — GIS Agent upholds three core values: placing users first and protecting their lawful rights; ensuring fairness and impartiality in rule enforcement; and maintaining security and control to safeguard platform information integrity.

### Scope of Application

These platform rules apply to all parties in the GIS Agent ecosystem, including all users, application creators, service providers, and platform operators.

---

## 2. Operational Compliance

### Content Publication and Licensing

If you create applications on the platform that require government filings or operating licenses (such as value-added telecommunications services), you must migrate those applications to a domain that already holds the appropriate approvals or licenses. The domain provided by GIS Agent is intended solely for temporary testing and preview of applications you build on the platform. If you plan to launch a commercial service publicly, you must transfer the application to your own licensed domain.

The platform reviews all applications published in the **App Showcase** section in accordance with these rules.

## 3. Prohibited Content Categories

<Warning>
  Platform content policies are enforced automatically and by human review. Violations may result in immediate content removal, feature restrictions, or permanent account termination without prior notice for severe violations.
</Warning>

Publishing any of the following types of prohibited content will result in the platform taking action against the relevant application or account. Severe violations will result in permanent account suspension.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="1. Violations of National Laws and Regulations">
    You must not create or publish any content that endangers national or social security. Prohibited content includes, but is not limited to:

    - Content that opposes the basic principles established by the Constitution
    - Content that endangers national security, divulges state secrets, subverts state power, or undermines national unity
    - Content that damages the national image, honor, or interests
    - Content that incites ethnic hatred or discrimination, or undermines ethnic solidarity
    - Content that violates national religious policies, or promotes cults, feudal superstitions, or pseudoscience
    - Content that incites unlawful assemblies, associations, marches, demonstrations, or gatherings that disrupt public order and social stability
    - Content that defames or libels others, or infringes upon others' reputation, privacy, or other lawful rights
    - Any other content prohibited by laws, administrative regulations, or national policy
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2. Criminal and Illegal Content">
    You must not create or publish content that promotes or facilitates illegal activity. Prohibited content includes, but is not limited to:

    - Content that promotes violence, terrorism, or extremism, or incites violent or terrorist acts
    - Content that distorts, vilifies, desecrates, or denies the deeds and spirit of heroes and martyrs
    - Content that uses national events, heroes, or martyrs for commercial marketing purposes
    - Content that spreads obscenity, pornography, gambling, drugs, violence, murder, terror, or that induces criminal behavior
    - Content that counterfeits, sells, maliciously damages, parodies, or defaces currency
    - Content that uses the platform to commit fraud, phishing, pyramid schemes, or smuggling
    - Content involving virtual currencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, or Tether (USDT)
    - Content that illegally promotes goods or services prohibited or restricted under applicable law, including but not limited to: firearms and ammunition, explosives, VPN circumvention tools, controlled knives, protected wildlife, unauthorized medical services, prescription drugs, counterfeit products, tobacco, counterfeit currency, stolen goods, smuggled items, illegal document services, and trafficking in human organs or gender identification services
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="3. Fraud and Impersonation">
    You must not create or publish fraudulent or impersonation content. Prohibited content includes, but is not limited to:

    - Illegal service schemes, such as fraudulent proxy services or other unlawful offerings
    - Identity impersonation, including posing as official government bodies, well-known companies, or other entities
    - Financial fraud, including impersonating financial institutions, loan fraud, investment fraud, or other financial scams
    - Fake transactions, such as fictitious product sales, fraudulent job postings, fake cashback schemes, or sham freelance gigs
    - Online romance scams, including dating app fraud, gaming friendship scams, or other relationship-based schemes
    - Developing or distributing applications designed to facilitate telecommunications fraud
    - Creating fake official websites that impersonate government agencies, military institutions, or official organizations
    - Any other fraud or impersonation scheme, including scams targeting specific demographic groups
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="4. Content Harmful to Minors">
    You must not create or publish any content that damages the physical or mental health, or lawful rights, of minors. Prohibited content includes, but is not limited to:

    - Sexual or vulgar content involving minors
    - Content depicting harm to minors' physical wellbeing, property, dignity, or privacy
    - Content portraying inappropriate behavior by minors
    - Content that corrupts minors' mental health or values
    - Content that poses potential risks or dangers to minors
    - Content that exploits minors for commercial or entertainment purposes
    - Content that incites or instigates fan-group conflicts ("fan wars") that lure minors into celebrity fan clubs or online activities that disrupt their normal study and rest schedules, or that organizes minors into online assemblies
    - Any other content or conduct that threatens minors' safety, health, or well-rounded development
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="5. Violent and Graphic Content">
    You must not create or publish content involving danger or that is likely to cause physical harm. Prohibited content includes, but is not limited to:

    - Content that spreads nihilistic or self-destructive sentiments, or that promotes a pessimistic worldview
    - Content that encourages self-harm, self-mutilation, or suicide, including graphic depictions of wounds, scars, or injuries, or suicide challenge games
    - Content involving graphic violence, blood, or horror in games, images, or other media that is deeply disturbing or distressing to viewers
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="6. Content That Infringes User Rights">
    You must not create or publish content that violates the principles of honesty and truthfulness. Prohibited content includes, but is not limited to:

    - Infringing others' personal rights, including defaming someone's reputation, misappropriating their likeness, or violating their privacy — for example, publishing someone's private information (such as home address, phone number, or financial details) without explicit consent
    - Infringing others' intellectual property rights, including patents, copyrights, or trademarks — for example, reproducing copyrighted materials or misappropriating trade secrets without authorization
    - Using others' sensitive personal information without proper anonymization, including: biometric data (unmasked facial images, fingerprints, iris scans, voiceprints), personal identity information (unauthorized ID numbers, passport numbers, phone numbers, home addresses, or bank accounts), medical and health records (diagnoses, medical histories, or genetic data), and any other sensitive data that could identify individuals, invade privacy, or lead to discrimination
    - Spreading false information, rumors, or other misleading or deceptive content
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="7. Content That Violates Public Order and Morality">
    You must not create or publish content that offends public order or social decency. Prohibited content includes, but is not limited to:

    - Soft pornography, vulgar content, sexual innuendo, or suggestive material; content that promotes lowbrow, mediocre, or crude culture
    - Content that promotes distorted views of marriage and romantic relationships, or that glorifies backward social practices such as crude wedding hazing
    - Content that mocks, discriminates against, or stereotypes people based on region, ethnicity, race, or disability status
    - Content that promotes conspicuous wealth, materialism, or a corrupt and decadent lifestyle
    - Content that violates others' privacy or exposes their personal information without consent
    - Inappropriate content involving animals, including animal abuse, illegal keeping of protected species, or illegal poaching and killing of animals
    - Content related to extreme fan behavior or fan-group culture, such as online feuds, mob attacks, harassment campaigns, organizing fan fundraising drives, doxxing, or exposing others' personal information
    - Content promoting extreme ideologies, including extreme forms of political activism or extremist animal rights advocacy
    - Any other content that violates public order, social ethics, or civilized conduct norms
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="8. Content That Damages Platform Interests">
    You must not create or publish content that undermines the platform's functionality, security, or user experience. Prohibited content and activities include, but are not limited to:

    - Uploading or publishing models, datasets, tools, scripts, documents, or related content intended to damage platform functionality or security — including spreading malware, viruses, or harmful code; unauthorized use of the bot API or automated abuse; cryptocurrency mining or other resource abuse; and sending spam, bulk unsolicited messages, or misleading links
    - Uploading or publishing models, or using the platform to make exaggerated, false, or misleading promotional claims, particularly in the medical, financial, or health supplement sectors
    - Uploading or publishing content that uses AI to conduct astroturfing or fake-account campaigns — for example, using AI to "cultivate" social media accounts, simulate real users to mass-register or operate fake accounts, generate low-quality duplicate content at scale to farm traffic, or use AI-driven bots to mass-like, comment, flood discussions, manipulate trending topics, or manufacture viral content
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="9. Other Illegal Content">
    Additional prohibited content categories include:

    - **AI technology misuse** — Content that explicitly references unethical or prohibited AI practices such as AI deepfake face-swapping, AI-powered undressing, AI resurrection of deceased individuals, or AI content laundering (rewriting content to evade detection)
    - **Unauthorized overseas model services** — Directly or indirectly providing access to AI model services from providers not registered within mainland China, including high-risk overseas large language model services from US-based companies (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, xAI, Meta AI)
    - **Prohibited activities on platform-hosted skill services** — Including:
      - *Cross-border circumvention services*: Providing any form of VPN, proxy, or web-scraping services to bypass the Great Firewall
      - *Services that harm cybersecurity*: Providing or facilitating cyberattacks, DDoS attacks, virus distribution, trojan injection, phishing, malicious scanning, port probing, vulnerability exploitation, or any other activity that disrupts the normal operation of network systems
      - *Illegal financial activity support*: Providing or facilitating unauthorized virtual currency transactions, illegal cross-border fund transfers, or any activity that supports illegal financial operations in violation of national foreign exchange management and financial regulatory policies
    - **Adversarial evasion content** — Any content that uses technical means — such as homophones, variant characters, symbols, images, encoding, or other obfuscation techniques — to circumvent the platform's content moderation systems in order to violate any of the rules above
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 4. Intellectual Property Protection

When you create applications or use content on the platform, you must ensure that your content does not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party. GIS Agent maintains an intellectual property infringement complaint mechanism in accordance with applicable law. The platform responds promptly to valid infringement notices submitted by rights holders. For applications or skills found to constitute infringement, the platform has the right to take necessary actions — including taking them offline, blocking access, or restricting functionality — to prevent the expansion of infringing harm and to protect the lawful rights of intellectual property holders.

## 5. Minor Protection

### Online Conduct and Content Publication

- **Healthy internet use** — Minor users should plan their usage time reasonably under the guidance of a guardian, avoid internet addiction, and develop good online habits. All users must comply with the *National Youth Internet Civility Compact* and use the service in a respectful, responsible manner.

- **Requirements for publishing content featuring minors** — If you publish content that includes the likeness, voice, or image of a minor, you must ensure you have obtained explicit authorization from the rights holder (i.e., the minor's legal guardian). By publishing such content, you confirm that you have the necessary authorization, that you consent to the platform using and processing the content in accordance with its terms, and that you will not infringe the lawful rights of the minor.

### Access Restrictions for Age-Inappropriate Content

For any application that contains content unsuitable for minors (content that may be harmful to their physical or mental development), you — as the publisher — must establish strict user admission and identity verification mechanisms. You must deploy reliable identity authentication technology across the registration, login, and usage flow to ensure the service is accessible only to adults with full civil capacity. You must not allow minors to bypass access controls to reach such content. The platform will take down or restrict the promotion of non-compliant applications and will hold publishers legally accountable in accordance with applicable law.

## 6. Monitoring and Enforcement

### Monitoring Mechanism

GIS Agent maintains a supervision and management system in accordance with applicable law. This includes reviewing and inspecting applications published on the platform, as well as operating a violation reporting and handling process. If you encounter any content or accounts that may violate platform rules while using the product, you can submit a report directly within the platform.

### Violation Handling

Violations of these rules are handled in accordance with Article 5 of the *GIS Agent User Agreement*.
