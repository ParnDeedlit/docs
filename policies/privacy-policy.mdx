This Privacy Policy explains how Wuhan Zhongdi Software Co., Ltd. (MapGIS) collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal information when you use GIS Agent services. Please read this policy carefully before using GIS Agent.

We take your personal information seriously. MapGIS is committed to complying with applicable laws and regulations and to adopting the appropriate security measures to protect your data. This policy helps you understand exactly what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

In addition to this policy, we may notify you of specific data collection practices through in-app pop-ups, page notices, and feature update announcements. Those notifications form part of this policy and carry the same effect.

**If you use GIS Agent to create and publish Agents, applications, or workflows for other users (including users on external platforms), you act as the personal information processor for those end users in your own name. The relationship between MapGIS and you in that capacity is governed by the GIS Agent Data Processing Agreement (contact MapGIS for the full agreement).**

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to the AI application services MapGIS provides to you through its website, app, mini-program, and other product forms. Where MapGIS or its affiliates maintain a separate privacy policy for a specific service, that separate policy governs. Features and services available in GIS Agent may vary across product versions; this policy covers only the data collection activities relevant to the features you actually use.

This policy does **not** apply to services provided to you by third parties — such as third-party SDKs or APIs integrated into GIS Agent, or services independently operated by third parties. Those third parties' own privacy policies govern the handling of your personal information in their services.

## 3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

### How We Use Cookies

To ensure the website works properly and to give you a smoother experience, we store small data files called cookies on your device. Cookies typically contain identifiers, the site name, and alphanumeric strings. We can only read cookies that we set.

We use cookies primarily to keep GIS Agent running securely and efficiently. Cookies help us confirm whether you are logged in safely, detect crashes and delays, and save you from re-entering search terms or filling in forms repeatedly.

### How to Manage Cookies

You can manage cookies according to your own preferences and delete all cookies stored on your device at any time. Most web browsers include options to block cookies. If you block cookies, you may need to adjust your settings each time you visit our website. For details, refer to your browser's help documentation or settings page.

### Other Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we use web beacons and pixel tags. Web beacons are typically transparent images embedded in web pages or emails. Pixel tags in emails allow us to know whether an email has been opened. If you do not want your activity tracked this way, you can unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time.

We commit to using cookies only for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

## 4. Sharing, Transfer, and Disclosure of Your Personal Information

### Core Principles

When we work with partners, we follow three guiding principles:

1. **Minimum necessary and lawful purpose** — Data processing must have a lawful basis and a legitimate purpose, and must be limited to the minimum scope required to achieve that purpose.
2. **Maximizing your right to know and decide** — We fully respect your right to be informed about and to make decisions about how your personal information is processed.
3. **Strong security safeguards** — We take necessary measures to safeguard the personal information we process, carefully evaluate partners' intended use of data, conduct comprehensive assessments of their security capabilities, and require them to comply with the terms of our data processing agreements.

### Authorized Partners

We may commission our affiliates or partners (collectively, **Authorized Partners**) to process your personal information on our behalf. We only commission such processing for the lawful, legitimate, necessary, specific, and explicit purposes described in this policy. Authorized Partners may only access the information they need to perform their specific functions, and we require — through agreement — that they may not use your information for any purpose beyond the scope of the commission. If an Authorized Partner wishes to use your information for any purpose we have not commissioned, they must obtain your separate consent.

Our current Authorized Partners include:

- **Advertising and analytics service providers** — We commission our affiliates and other Authorized Partners, using industry-standard security technologies, to process information related to marketing, advertising placement, reach, and effectiveness.
- **Product and service vendors and other partners** — We commission affiliates and other Authorized Partners to handle information in support of our business operations, including consulting, analytics, marketing, and technical support services carried out on our behalf.
- **Payment service providers** — To complete order payments, we share your account information and order details with payment Authorized Partners to process the transaction.

### Conditions for Disclosure

We may publicly disclose your personal information only under the following circumstances:

- With your explicit consent
- When required by law — for example, to comply with a court order, administrative investigation, or other mandatory legal process
- When necessary to protect the vital interests of you or others in urgent circumstances

In all cases, we will disclose only the minimum information required and will notify you where permitted by law.

## 6. Data Storage and Protection

### Data Storage Location and Retention Period

Your personal information is stored on servers located within the People's Republic of China. We retain your personal information for the period necessary to provide our services and to comply with applicable legal obligations. The specific retention period varies by information type:

- **Account information** is retained for the duration of your account's active life, plus any additional period required by law after account cancellation.
- **Conversation and interaction records** are retained until you actively delete them or until your account is cancelled.
- **Log and security data** is retained for the period required under network security laws and relevant regulations.

When the retention period expires and no legal obligation requires us to continue holding the data, we will delete or anonymize it.

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include:

- Encrypted transmission using HTTPS/TLS protocols
- Encrypted storage of sensitive data
- Strict access controls and employee confidentiality obligations
- Regular security assessments and audits
- Incident response procedures for personal data breaches

In the event of a personal data security incident, we will notify you and the relevant authorities in accordance with applicable law.

## 7. Special Provisions for Minors

GIS Agent is not intended for users under the age of 14. **We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 14.** If you are under 14, please do not use GIS Agent or provide any personal information to us.

Users between the ages of 14 and 18 must obtain the consent of a parent or legal guardian before using GIS Agent. Parents and guardians are responsible for guiding minors in their use of the service.

If you are a parent or guardian and discover that a minor in your care has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately using the information in Section 9. We will take prompt steps to delete the relevant information.

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a user under the age of 14, we will delete that information as soon as possible.
