GIS Agent is MapGIS's AI-powered geospatial intelligence platform that lets anyone describe a geographic task in plain language and receive a complete, deployable GIS application. Whether you need to model terrain, analyze urban infrastructure, or publish a real-time disaster response map, GIS Agent handles the full workflow automatically. Its key differentiator is deep integration with professional GIS tools, spatiotemporal databases, and Chinese GIS standards — including native support for CGCS2000, Beijing 54, and Xi'an 80 coordinate systems.

## What GIS Agent Does

GIS Agent runs your entire geospatial workflow end-to-end: it ingests data from files, databases, and online services; performs spatial analysis using 115+ built-in GIS tools; renders professional-grade maps with automatic symbology and layer configuration; and publishes the result as a shareable web application or standard service endpoint (WMS/WFS). You describe the goal in plain language — GIS Agent executes every step, from raw data to a live, interactive map.

## Who Uses GIS Agent

GIS entrepreneurs and developers use GIS Agent to prototype and ship spatial applications in hours rather than weeks. Industry analysts, business decision-makers, and government managers use it to generate on-demand spatial reports and situation maps that feed directly into planning and emergency response. Map designers and visualization experts use it to bring high-fidelity visual concepts to life as interactive, data-driven applications without writing a single line of code. Enterprise and government teams use it to standardize GIS workflows into reusable Skills, preserving institutional knowledge and slashing the cost of every future deployment.
